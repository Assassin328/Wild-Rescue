Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mission of Wild Rescues?

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release wildlife back into their natural environment.

How can I volunteer?

You can apply to become a volunteer on our Partnership page.

What types of animals do you rescue?

We rescue a variety of wildlife including birds, mammals, and reptiles.

Can I visit the animals?

Visits are limited, but you can follow us on social media for updates and events.
